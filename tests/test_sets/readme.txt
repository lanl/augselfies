The dataset is a variant of the data presented at 
https://springernature.figshare.com/articles/dataset/Data_for_6095_constitutional_isomers_of_C7H10O2/1057646?file=3195389,

Ramakrishnan, R. et al. Quantum chemistry structures and properties of 134 kilo molecules. Sci. Data 1:140022 doi: 10.1038/sdata.2014.22 (2014).
